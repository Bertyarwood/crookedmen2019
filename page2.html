Dave West
Dave is generally quite affable but sometimes gets cross. Because of this he is also known as Mr Grumpy. Will he ever lose this tag?







Martin Wilkes
Fred plays a steady game, always in the mix. Consistently Mr Average. Also known as the Saint.







John Cookson
John loves his golf but loves his wine even more. A good vintage player is Mr Wine.







Steve West
Steve is a rival for the Mr Holiday title. Maybe you have to be called Steve to go away for so many holidays. For the time being he will still be called Junior.







Steve Dewar
I asked this question last year: "would he perform better if his season is not so interrupted by holidays?"  His emphatic win in Norfolk gives the answer. Will he lose is Mr Holiday title to his younger namesake?







Andrew Burley
Andrew has the lowest handicap in the field and impressed throughout 2018.  It will be good to see how the man with the golden swing, Mr Bond, performs this year .







Nigel Mills
One of the most entertaining players on the tour, A pleasure to partner - 'cos he usually has some flapjacks. Look out for Bomber Mills unleashing a big one.







John McKeon
Winner of the 2015 French tour and in the mix until the final round in Norfolk, John's been practising eating and drinking in Spain as usual. Always courteous on and off the course, Mr Charming has the game to trouble the leaderboard.







Peter Hoghton
